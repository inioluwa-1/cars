Lewis Hamilton pleased with 'beautiful'
third for Spanish GP after qualifying
breakthrough and now eyes win

"I am going into [the race] to try and win.... we are in a beautiful spot," says Lewis Hamilton
after qualifying season-best third in Mercedes' latest sign of progress; watch the Spanish
Grand Prix at 2pm, with build-up from 12.30pm, live on Sky Sports F1 on Sunday


Saturday 22 June 2024 20:29, UK



Lewis Hamilton said Mercedes' return to contending at the front of
the F1 grid had been a "long time coming" after they locked out the
second row at the Spanish Grand Prix and he delivered the best
qualifying result of his disappointing season.


The seven-time world champion will start inside the top four for the
first time in 15 races and is not ruling out what would be a first race
win since December 2021.
